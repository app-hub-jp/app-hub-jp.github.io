FAQ

Q. How do I record a set?

A. On the Log screen, choose a category and exercise, adjust the weight and reps, then swipe up in the number area to save the set. Reps must be at least 1.

Q. Where can I view my records?

A. Open Today to see today’s sets, or open History and select a date to see the sets recorded for that day.

Q. How do I edit or delete a recorded set?

A. Tap a set in Today or History to show the weight and rep controls. To delete a set, swipe it to the left and select the delete action.

Q. Can I add a set to an earlier date?

A. Yes. Open History, select today or an earlier date, and use Add to add another set or exercise. Records cannot be added to a future date.

Q. How do I customize categories, exercises, units, or increments?

A. Open the menu and select Categories, Exercises, Unit, or Increments. Deleting a category or exercise does not delete workout sets that were already recorded.

Q. When does free access begin?

A. Free access begins when you select Start and lasts for 14 days from that time.

Q. What happens when free access ends?

A. Lifetime Access is required to add new workout records. You can still view your existing records.

Q. Is Lifetime Access a subscription?

A. No. It is a one-time purchase, and you will not be charged automatically.

Q. How do I restore a purchase?

A. Open Unlock from the menu and select Restore Purchases while using the Apple Account that made the original purchase.

Q. Where is my workout data stored?

A. Your workout records and settings are stored locally on your device. CherryTomatoes has no account or cloud-sync service. Depending on your device settings, app data may be included in an iOS device backup or transferred with Quick Start.
